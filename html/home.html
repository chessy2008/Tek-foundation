Tek Foundation

No one should go to bed hungry or live in fear. We are on a
mission to end food scarcity, insecurity and promote
peace through community-driven solutions.

Our Vision

To envision a society where no woman is held back by hunger, poverty, or lack of opportunity. Where every woman, no matter where she comes from, has what she needs to live with dignity, strength, and peace of mind.

Our Mission

To address poverty and food insecurity by equipping underserved women with sustainable solutions that promote economic independence, improved nutrition, and a healthier quality of life.

Our Impact

With the support of our partners and volunteers, we’re reaching communities across Nigeria.

  • 100+ Children Educated
  • 30 Community Projects
  • 50k+ Meals Served
  • 12+ Active Volunteers
