Who We Are

TEK Foundation is a non-governmental organization empowering underserved women in rural communities with practical tools to break the cycle of poverty and hunger. Through training, resources, and support, we help women build self-reliant lives and stronger communities

History

TEK Foundation was founded in 2022, born out of a deep desire to serve those who were struggling—especially women, children, and low-income families. Our very first outreach happened in December 2022, when we distributed food to families during the festive season. It was a simple gesture, but its impact was profound.

What began as a one-time initiative quickly revealed a larger calling. We witnessed the harsh realities of hunger—how it steals hope, weakens families, and keeps dreams out of reach. We saw mothers skipping meals so their children could eat, and families trapped in cycles of poverty with no way out.

That was the moment TEK Foundation became more than an idea. It became a mission. With hearts full of compassion and faith in God’s purpose, we set out to fight hunger, uplift the poor, and restore dignity to the forgotten. Every outreach, every training, every act of kindness is our way of saying: You are seen. You are loved. You are not alone.

Our Vision

To envision a society where no woman is held back by hunger,poverty, or lack of opportunity. Where every woman,no matter where she comes from, has what she needs to live with dignity, strength, and peace of mind.

Our Mission

To address poverty and food insecurity by equipping underserved women with sustainable solutions that promote
economic independence, improved nutrition,
and a healthier quality of life.

Our Goals & Objectives

A roadmap to the change we strive to create in the lives of women and communities.

Reduce Poverty

Support women with tools to escape the cycle of poverty and build sustainable livelihoods.

Train 500 Women

Equip women in rural areas with practical vocational skills and mentorship by 2026.

Access to Education

Promote lifelong learning, literacy, and digital inclusion for underserved women.

Build 3 Centers

Establish 3 community development centers to provide learning and support services.

Improve Health & Nutrition

Empower families with better nutrition, hygiene education, and access to resources.

Monthly Food Drives

Deliver nutritious food packages to families in partnership with local farmers and donors.
